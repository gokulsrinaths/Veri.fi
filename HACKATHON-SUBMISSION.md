Summary

veri.fi turns real-world activity into programmable proof. Sponsors create tasks and escrow rewards on Creditcoin testnet; participants submit photo proof; our backend scores each submission (visual, location, timestamp, anti-fraud) and the smart contract releases the reward when verification passes. Built for BUIDL CTC — Build for the Real World. Fits DePIN (verify infrastructure), RWA (attest real-world state), and DeFi (escrow and settlement on Creditcoin). Tech: Next.js, TypeScript, Tailwind, Supabase, VeriActEscrow on Creditcoin testnet, DeepInfra/OpenAI for vision. Run: pnpm install, pnpm dev:web. veri.fi — Prove it. Get paid.
